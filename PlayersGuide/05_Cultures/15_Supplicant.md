## Supplicant

You were raised in a community bound to serve a powerful and dangerous ruler. Your people may have lived under a dragon, giant lord, cruel sorcerer, fiendish patron, subterranean tyrant, or some other being whose will shaped every part of daily life. Some supplicant communities served from fear, some from dependence, some from old custom, and some because survival seemed to leave no better choice.

Life in such a community teaches caution, quick movement, and constant attention to power. You learn how to read moods, navigate dangerous hierarchies, and avoid becoming the easiest target when tempers rise. Even after leaving such a life behind, you may still carry the habits of a survivor who learned to live close to peril.

### Supplicant Traits

#### Scurry

As a bonus action, you can move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

This movement does not trigger traps or hazards that you are aware of, even if they are armed.

Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.

#### Supplicant

You have proficiency in either the Insight or Persuasion skill.

In addition, when a creature you can see within 30 feet of you hits you or one of your allies with an attack, you have advantage on your next ability check or saving throw made before the end of your next turn.

#### Languages

You know Common and one additional language of your choice.

Supplicant communities often learn the language favored by their current or former rulers, enforcers, or neighboring powers.
