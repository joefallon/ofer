## Collegiate Traits

### Philosophic Mind

Once per long rest, at the start of your turn, you can suppress the effects of one enchantment spell affecting you until the start of your next turn.

### Practiced Artisan

You have proficiency with calligrapher's supplies or one artisan's tool of your choice.

### Studied Discipline

Choose one of the following disciplines:

**Arts and Letters.** You gain proficiency in History or Performance.

**Lore.** You gain proficiency in Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion.

**Medicine.** You gain proficiency in Medicine.

**Reason and Rhetoric.** You gain proficiency in Insight or Persuasion.

### Languages

You know Common and two additional languages of your choice.

Collegiate communities often teach the languages most useful for scholarship, liturgy, diplomacy, trade, or older written traditions.
