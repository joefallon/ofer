## Grove

You were raised in a woodland community shaped by the rhythms, hazards, and gifts of the forest. Sacred groves, hidden villages, canopy settlements, deep green borderlands, and other forest homes all produce grove communities.

Life in such places teaches balance, patience, and close attention to the natural world. You learn how to move through branches and undergrowth, how to use rain, mist, and foliage as cover, and how to live with the land rather than forcing it into submission. Even when far from the woods, you may still carry the quiet habits of the grove with you.

### Grove Traits

#### Canopy Walker

You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

#### Nature's Camouflage

You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made while you are lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.

While in such conditions, you can always attempt to take the Hide action, even if circumstances would not normally allow you to do so.

#### Languages

You know Common and one additional language of your choice.

Typical grove characters often learn the language of nearby forest peoples, druidic circles, ancient local traditions, or long-standing woodland neighbors.
