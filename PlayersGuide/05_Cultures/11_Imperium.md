## Imperium

You were raised within the reach of a large, organized state whose laws, institutions, and customs shaped daily life. Great capitals, disciplined kingdoms, deep hold-realms, old dynastic cities, and other centers of long-standing authority all produce imperium cultures.

Life in such places teaches order, routine, and trust in systems larger than any one household. You grow up around schools, officials, guards, healers, records, and public expectations that reach across whole regions. Even where wealth and status vary sharply, people raised in imperium communities are often shaped by structured education, civic hierarchy, and the belief that stability is maintained through institutions.

### Imperium Traits

#### Conscript

You gain proficiency with one martial weapon of your choice.

#### Learned Teachers

You gain proficiency in the History skill and one other skill of your choice.

#### Local Healers

Whenever your hit point maximum or one of your ability scores would be reduced, it is reduced by half as much instead, to a minimum reduction of 1.

#### Languages

You know Common and one additional language of your choice.

Imperium communities often teach the languages most useful for administration, trade, diplomacy, military service, or communication across the wider state.
