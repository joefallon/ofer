## Diaspora

You were raised in a community shaped by displacement, migration, and the effort to preserve its identity far from an older homeland. Your people may have been driven out by war, disaster, oppression, collapse, or slow generations of scattering, but they carried their memory, customs, and obligations with them.

Life in such a community teaches endurance. You learn the value of preserving stories, guarding traditions, building alliances, and standing firm beside your own when fear and uncertainty press in. Diaspora communities often become skilled at adapting to new lands without surrendering the truths they consider worth keeping.

### Diaspora Traits

#### Preserved Traditions

You have proficiency in the History skill.

You also gain proficiency with one type of martial weapon of your choice.

#### Timeless Resolve

When you or an allied creature within 5 feet of you makes a saving throw against becoming frightened, you and the ally have advantage on the saving throw.

#### Languages

You know Common and one additional language of your choice.

Many diaspora communities learn the languages most common among their neighbors, trading partners, soldiers, or rivals.
