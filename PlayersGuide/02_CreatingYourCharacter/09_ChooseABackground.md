## Choose a Background

Your character’s **background** represents the life they lived before becoming an adventurer. It helps explain what your character used to do, what they learned, and what still drives them now.

A background can shape your character in practical ways by granting things like:

- **skill proficiencies**
- **tool proficiencies**
- **languages**
- **a talent**
- **starting equipment**
- **an adventuring motivation**

Your background also helps answer important character questions like:

- **What kind of life did your character live before adventuring?**
- **What useful training or experience did they gain from that life?**
- **Why did they leave that life behind?**
- **What now drives them into danger, discovery, or service?**

A good background adds both mechanical benefits and roleplaying depth. It helps connect your character’s past to the person they are now.
