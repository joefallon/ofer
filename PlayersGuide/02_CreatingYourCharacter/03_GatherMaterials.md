## Gather Materials

Before you start creating your character, make sure you have a few basic tools ready. Having everything in front of you will make the process much easier.

You should have the following:

- **a pencil**
- **a blank character sheet**
- **scratch paper**
- **dice**

Each of these helps with a different part of character creation:

- **A pencil** lets you write down your choices and make changes as needed.
- **A blank character sheet** gives you a place to record all the important details about your character.
- **Scratch paper** is useful for temporary notes, quick calculations, and trying out ideas before writing them onto your character sheet.
- **Dice** are needed for play, and they may also be needed during character creation if your group uses the rolling method for ability scores.

If you do not have printed materials, digital versions can work just as well as long as you can easily record and update your character information.
