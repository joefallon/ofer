## Choose a Lineage

Your character’s **lineage** represents the traits they are born with or inherit. This includes things like **size**, **speed**, **senses**, and other natural qualities.

Your lineage helps answer questions like:

- **What is your character physically like?**
- **What natural traits do they have?**
- **What inherited advantages or adaptations do they bring with them?**

Choose your lineage from the options presented in **Chapter 4: Lineages**.
