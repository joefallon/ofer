## Choose a Culture

Your character’s **culture** represents the culture, community, and way of life that shaped them as they grew up. While lineage describes inherited traits, culture describes the learned traits, knowledge, and customs your character gained from their upbringing.

Your culture helps answer questions like:

- **Where was your character raised?**
- **What kind of community shaped them?**
- **What skills, habits, or cultural knowledge did they learn growing up?**

A culture may affect things like:

- **skills**
- **languages**
- **practical knowledge**
- **learned cultural traits**

Choose your culture from the options presented in **Chapter 5: Cultures**.
