## Creating Your Character

The first step in play is creating your player character (PC).

Character creation is where you decide who your character is, what they are good at, and how they fit into the adventuring party. Some of these choices are rules decisions, such as your class and ability scores. Others are creative decisions, such as your character’s background, personality, appearance, and goals.

This chapter walks you through character creation one step at a time. If this is your first time making a character, your game master (GM) can help you understand your options and make choices that will be fun and easy to play.

In this chapter, you will:

- **gather your materials**
- **create a character concept**
- **choose a class**
- **determine your ability scores**
- **choose a lineage**
- **choose a heritage**
- **choose a background**
- **determine your starting equipment**
- **fill in the remaining details on your character sheet**

By the end of this chapter, you will have a complete character ready for play.
