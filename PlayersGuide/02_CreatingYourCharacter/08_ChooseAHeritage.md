## Choose a Heritage

Your character’s **heritage** represents the culture, community, and way of life that shaped them as they grew up. While lineage describes inherited traits, heritage describes the learned traits, knowledge, and customs your character gained from their upbringing.

Your heritage helps answer questions like:

- **Where was your character raised?**
- **What kind of community shaped them?**
- **What skills, habits, or cultural knowledge did they learn growing up?**

A heritage may affect things like:

- **skills**
- **languages**
- **practical knowledge**
- **learned cultural traits**

Choose your heritage from the options presented in **Chapter 5: Heritages**.
