## Final Checklist

Before play begins, review your character sheet and make sure you have recorded everything you need.

Use this checklist to catch any missing details.

### 1. Your class and class features

Make sure you have written down your:

- **class**
    
- **class features**
    
- **hit die**
    
- **proficiency bonus**
    
- **saving throw proficiencies**
    
- **skill proficiencies**
    
- **weapon proficiencies**
    
- **armor proficiencies**
    
- **tool proficiencies**, if any
    
- **spellcasting information**, if applicable
    

If your character can cast spells, make sure you also have your:

- **cantrips**
    
- **spells known or prepared**
    
- **spell slots**
    
- **spell save DC**
    
- **spell attack modifier**
    

### 2. Your ability scores and derived statistics

Make sure your ability scores are assigned and that you have used them to calculate your main numbers.

Check that you have recorded your:

- **Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores**
    
- **ability modifiers**
    
- **maximum hit points**
    
- **armor class**
    
- **initiative**
    
- **speed**
    
- **attack bonuses**
    
- **damage bonuses**
    
- **saving throw bonuses**
    
- **skill bonuses**
    

If your character can cast spells, double-check your spellcasting numbers as well.

### 3. Your lineage

Make sure you have recorded everything your lineage gives you, including your:

- **size**
    
- **speed**
    
- **senses**
    
- **special lineage traits**
    
- **additional proficiencies**, if any
    

### 4. Your heritage

Make sure you have recorded everything your heritage gives you, including any:

- **skills**
    
- **languages**
    
- **learned traits**
    
- **cultural or practical benefits**
    
- **other heritage features**
    

### 5. Your background

Make sure you have recorded everything your background gives you, including your:

- **skill proficiencies**
    
- **tool proficiencies**, if any
    
- **languages**
    
- **talent**
    
- **starting equipment**
    
- **adventuring motivation**
    

### 6. Your starting equipment

Make sure you have written down all of your starting gear, including your:

- **weapons**
    
- **armor**
    
- **tools**
    
- **adventuring gear**
    
- **packs or kits**
    
- **coins or unspent gold**
    

### 7. Your personal details

Make sure your character has the basic details needed to bring them to life.

At minimum, record your:

- **name**
    
- **appearance**
    
- **personality**
    
- **background details**
    
- **reason for adventuring**
    

You may also want to note details such as:

- **height**
    
- **weight**
    
- **age**
    
- **hair color**
    
- **eye color**
    
- **distinguishing features**
    

### 8. Luck

Make sure your **Luck** is set to **0**, unless a rule says otherwise.

### 9. Final review

Before you are done, ask yourself:

- **Can I tell what my character is good at?**
    
- **Can I quickly find my most important numbers?**
    
- **Have I written down the features and abilities I will need during play?**
    
- **Is anything important still blank?**
    

If everything above is complete, your character is ready to play.
