## Welcome

Welcome to a game of fantasy adventure.

Dangerous frontiers, lost places, strange powers, and hidden truths await discovery. Some journeys lead into ruins and wilderness, while others lead into courts, strongholds, battlefields, or realms touched by magic. Whatever form the adventure takes, the actions of the characters matter. Their decisions, risks, and victories help shape the world around them.

## What Is This Game?

This is a fantasy tabletop roleplaying game.

In play:

* **The players** take the roles of adventurers.
* **The characters** explore the world, confront threats, overcome obstacles, and pursue goals together.
* **One participant serves as the game master**, describing the world, portraying its people and dangers, and judging outcomes when the rules leave room for interpretation.

Play moves forward through:

* **conversation**
* **player choice**
* **imagined action**
* **dice and rules** used to resolve uncertain outcomes

## Core Idea

At its heart, this game is about a group of adventurers entering a world of danger and possibility, making meaningful decisions, and living with the results.

The world presents opportunities, mysteries, and threats.

The characters respond.

The story emerges from play.
