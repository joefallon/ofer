## Tiefling

### Overview

Tieflings bear the mark of fiendish influence somewhere in their lineage. Horns, unusual eyes, strange shadows, ember-warm skin, a voice with an unsettling edge, or other uncanny signs often reveal that they are not entirely of the mortal world. Some tieflings descend from infernal bargains or ancient bloodlines, while others inherit stranger legacies tied to darker forces below the world or beyond it.

A tiefling character is a strong choice for players who want a lineage that feels uncanny, magical, and marked by dangerous supernatural lineage.

### Lineage Traits

Your tiefling character has the following traits.

#### Creature Type

You are a Humanoid.

#### Size

You are Medium or Small. Choose your size when you select this lineage.

#### Speed

Your walking speed is 30 feet.

#### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

#### Otherworldly Presence

You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip.

Choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as your spellcasting ability for this trait when you select this lineage.

### Fiendish Legacy

Choose one of the following Fiendish Legacies: Abyssal, Chthonic, or Infernal. You gain all traits listed under the legacy you choose.

#### Abyssal

Your lineage is touched by chaos, corruption, and the cruelty of the Abyss.

**Poison Resistance**

You have resistance to poison damage.

**Poison Spray**

You know the Poison Spray cantrip.

**Sickening Magic**

You can cast Ray of Sickness once without using a spell slot. Once you cast it in this way, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.

#### Chthonic

Your lineage is tied to darkness, death, and powers that linger in the deep places of the world.

**Necrotic Resistance**

You have resistance to necrotic damage.

**Chill Touch**

You know the Chill Touch cantrip.

**Grave-Touched Vitality**

You can cast False Life once without using a spell slot. Once you cast it in this way, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.

#### Infernal

Your lineage is marked by hellfire, bargains, and infernal blood.

**Fire Resistance**

You have resistance to fire damage.

**Fire Bolt**

You know the Fire Bolt cantrip.

**Hellish Rebuke**

You can cast Hellish Rebuke once without using a spell slot. Once you cast it in this way, you cannot do so again until you finish a long rest.

Choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as your spellcasting ability for the spells granted by your Fiendish Legacy when you select this lineage.

### Playing a Tiefling

Tieflings often suit characters defined by intensity, independence, secrecy, temptation, defiance, or the effort to live under the weight of other people’s expectations. Some embrace their strange lineage, some hide it, and some treat it as nothing more than a fact of birth. One tiefling might be a charming rogue with ember-bright eyes, another a grim occult scholar, another a hot-tempered warrior marked by hellfire, and another a quiet wanderer who has spent a lifetime being judged before speaking a word.

When playing a tiefling, think about questions like these:

- How visibly does your fiendish lineage show itself?
- Do you treat your lineage as a burden, a weapon, a mystery, or a private fact?
- Has the world feared you, tempted you, or tried to define you before you could define yourself?
- What do you believe about the power in your blood?

Tieflings are often at their best when they feel vivid, haunted, and impossible to mistake for ordinary.
