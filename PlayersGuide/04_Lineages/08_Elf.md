## Elf

### Overview

Elves are graceful, perceptive, and touched by something otherworldly. They are often associated with beauty, patience, artistry, and old magic, but elves are not defined by a single path. Some are mystics, wanderers, hunters, scholars, seers, or guardians. What unites them is a sense of refinement and awareness that can seem uncanny to shorter-lived peoples.

An elf character is a strong choice for players who want a lineage that feels elegant, alert, magical, and subtly supernatural.

### Lineage Traits

Your elf character has the following traits.

#### Creature Type

You are a Humanoid.

#### Size

You are Medium.

#### Speed

Your walking speed is 30 feet.

#### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

#### Keen Senses

You gain proficiency in Insight, Perception, or Survival. Choose one when you select this lineage.

#### Fey Ancestry

You have advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the Charmed condition, and magic cannot put you to sleep.

#### Trance

You do not need to sleep. When you take a long rest, you can spend 4 hours in a deep trance instead of sleeping. During this trance, you remain conscious. You still gain the full benefits of the long rest as normal.

### Elf Gift

Choose one of the following Elf Gifts: Mystic Rapport, Prescient Vision, or Preternatural Awareness. You gain all traits listed under the gift you choose.

#### Mystic Rapport

You are especially attuned to magic, thought, and subtle connections between living minds.

**Arcane Sensitivity**

You gain proficiency in Arcana.

**Arcane Empathy**

You can speak telepathically to one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The creature does not gain the ability to respond telepathically, but it can understand you if you share a language.

#### Prescient Vision

Your awareness reaches a little ahead of the present, letting you glimpse what may come next.

**Glance the Future**

Once per long rest, as a bonus action, you roll a d20 and record the result.

Before the end of your next long rest, when a creature you can see within 60 feet makes an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, you can use your reaction to replace the d20 roll with your recorded result.

Once you use this trait, the recorded result is lost.

#### Preternatural Awareness

Your instincts are sharp enough to seem supernatural.

**Keen Perception**

You gain proficiency in Perception. If you already have proficiency in Perception, you instead gain proficiency in Insight or Survival.

**Prophetic Instincts**

You gain a bonus to initiative rolls equal to your Wisdom modifier, to a minimum bonus of 1.

While you are conscious, you cannot be surprised.

### Playing an Elf

Elves often suit characters defined by poise, sensitivity, discipline, curiosity, or quiet intensity. Some feel deeply tied to beauty, memory, magic, or the natural world, while others are shaped by exile, scholarship, war, wandering, or spiritual calling. One elf might be a patient archer and guide, another a courtly mage, another a haunted seer, and another a silent scout whose attention never seems to waver.

When playing an elf, think about questions like these:

- What gives you your sense of distance from or closeness to the world around you?
- Does your character feel ancient in outlook, youthful in curiosity, or something in between?
- Is your connection to the uncanny a comfort, a burden, or simply part of life?
- Do others see you as graceful and wise, or remote and unreadable?

Elves are often at their best when they feel observant, composed, and slightly beyond the ordinary.
