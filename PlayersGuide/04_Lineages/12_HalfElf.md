## Half-Elf

### Overview

Half-elves stand between worlds. They often inherit some of the grace and sensitivity of elves along with the adaptability and restless drive of humans, but no two half-elves express that heritage in exactly the same way. Some move easily between different communities, while others feel caught between them. Some are poised and diplomatic, some sharp-eyed and reserved, and some independent souls who learn to define themselves on their own terms.

A half-elf character is a strong choice for players who want a lineage that feels versatile, perceptive, and shaped by more than one inheritance.

### Lineage Traits

Your half-elf character has the following traits.

#### Creature Type

You are a Humanoid.

#### Size

You are Medium.

#### Speed

Your walking speed is 30 feet.

#### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

#### Fey Ancestry

You have advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the Charmed condition, and magic cannot put you to sleep.

#### Skill Versatility

You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.

### Half-Elf Gift

Choose one of the following Half-Elf Gifts: Diplomatic Bearing, Keen Heritage, or Restless Versatility. You gain all traits listed under the gift you choose.

#### Diplomatic Bearing

You are especially gifted at moving between people, cultures, and expectations.

**Social Grace**

You gain proficiency in Deception, Insight, Persuasion, or Performance. Choose one when you select this gift.

**Bridging Presence**

When you make a Charisma check to calm tension, negotiate, make peace, or improve a creature’s attitude toward you, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the check.

You can use this trait once per long rest.

#### Keen Heritage

Your elven blood shows itself most clearly in your senses and awareness.

**Keen Senses**

You gain proficiency in Perception. If you already have proficiency in Perception, you instead gain proficiency in Insight or Survival.

**Watchful**

You gain a bonus to initiative rolls equal to your Wisdom modifier, to a minimum bonus of 1.

#### Restless Versatility

Your human side shows itself in determination, flexibility, and a talent for learning quickly.

**Adaptable**

You gain proficiency with one tool of your choice or one additional skill of your choice.

**Driven**

Once per long rest, when you make an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the result. You can decide to use this trait after rolling the d20, but before the outcome is determined.

### Playing a Half-Elf

Half-elves often suit characters defined by adaptability, ambiguity, sensitivity, or the search for belonging. Some feel at home in many places, while others never fully belong anywhere. One half-elf might be a warm and gifted negotiator, another a solitary wanderer with sharp senses, and another a driven adventurer who learned early to rely on flexibility and force of will.

When playing a half-elf, think about questions like these:

- Which side of your heritage is most visible to others, and which matters most to you?
- Do you feel like a bridge between worlds, or like someone expected to choose between them?
- Have you embraced your mixed heritage, hidden it, or built a new identity from it?
- Does your character seek belonging, independence, recognition, or something else entirely?

Half-elves are often at their best when they feel poised, adaptable, and a little difficult to place.
